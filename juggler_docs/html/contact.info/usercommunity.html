Other VR Web Sites:

  • vrsource.org - a good site for unbiased news and discussion of VR, graphics, game topics.
  • Vis-Sim.org - a good site for unbiased news and discussion of vis/sim topics.
  • Virtual Reality Association Inc. - a non-profit International Association dedicated to furthering the art and science of VR through community involvement.
  • Virtual Reality "Society" - a resource for any with an interest in Virtual Reality.
  • VR.EDU - Educational site on VR and computer graphics. Contains most comprehensive conference listing known to man; hosts a number of related conferences; and is a repository for tutorials about VR technology.
  • CALT Encyclopedia -- Virtual Reality - Huge collection of resources related to Virtual Environments, VR, Gaming, Muds, etc.
  • Tommys Page - a fairly complete listing of VR sites.
