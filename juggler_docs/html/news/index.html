June 19-20, 2000
 VR Juggler Shows off at IPT2000

   
 

This year's Immersive Projection Technology Workshop (IPT 2000) unveiled VRAC's new 6 sided cave-like device named the C6. To show off this new system, applications written on top of the Juggler virtual platform (about 16 in all) were demonstrated for the 300+ attendees. Everything was a success: framerate was high, crashes were non-existant... . . .

January 27, 2000
 VR Juggler version 0.1.95 released as OpenSource

   
 

VR Juggler, the virtual platform for VR application development, has been opensourced under the LGPL licence.  VR developers from around the world can now work together to evolve VR Juggler into the standard platform for VR application development.
