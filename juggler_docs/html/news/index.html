July 20, 2000
 VR Juggler now on Sourceforge.net

We have created a Juggler webpage on sourceforge.net as a way to bring you access to forums, messaging, and bug tracking for the VR Juggler virtual platform. From their site: "SourceForge's mission is to enrich the Open Source community by providing a centralized place for Open Source Developers to control and manage Open Source Software Development."
NOTE: CVS access is not yet available via sourceforge, we are in the process of testing this feature and will make another announcement when the Juggler sourceforge site includes CVS access.

 VR Juggler version 0.1.97 released

After much anticipation by all, the newest release of VR Juggler has been released just in time for SIGGRAPH 2000. The newest version 0.1.97 contains feature additions, bugfixes, and performance increases. Download the latest version.

June 19-20, 2000
 VR Juggler Shows off at IPT2000

   
 

This year's Immersive Projection Technology Workshop (IPT 2000) unveiled VRAC's new 6 sided cave-like device named the C6. To show off this new system, applications written on top of the Juggler virtual platform (about 22 in all) were demonstrated for the 300+ attendees. Everything was a success: framerate was high, crashes were non-existent... . . .

January 27, 2000
 VR Juggler version 0.1.95 released as OpenSource

   
 

VR Juggler, the virtual platform for VR application development, has been opensourced under the LGPL licence.  VR developers from around the world can now work together to evolve VR Juggler into the standard platform for VR application development.
