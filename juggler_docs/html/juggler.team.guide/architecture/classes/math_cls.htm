VR Juggler defines several mathmatical primitive for it's uses.  These primitives are just simple vectors, matrices, and quaternions.

Data returned from VR Juggler is returned in these formats.  Users may also use these primitive in their own code, or simply copy the data into their own structures.

vjMatrix: a 4x4 matrix

vjVec3: A point/ray vector

vjQuat: A quaternion

vjCoord: A coordinate. (postion and orientation)
